About RootsStay Platform

RootsStay is a revolutionary platform dedicated to promoting rural tourism and empowering local communities across India. We bridge the gap between adventurous travelers seeking authentic experiences and rural families eager to share their heritage and hospitality. By booking through RootsStay, you not only embark on an unforgettable journey but also directly contribute to the sustainable development and economic upliftment of rural areas. Our mission is to preserve cultural traditions, support local economies, and offer a unique glimpse into the heart of rural India.

About Our Team

We are a dedicated team of student developers passionate about creating technology that makes a tangible social impact. Through RootsStay, our goal is to empower rural communities, foster cultural preservation, and enable sustainable tourism across India. We strive to achieve this by building a user-friendly and inclusive digital platform. We firmly believe that technology can effectively bridge the rural and urban divide in India, and RootsStay represents a significant step towards realizing that transformative vision.
